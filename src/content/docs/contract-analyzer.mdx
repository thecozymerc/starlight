# Contract Analyzer (Beta)
This tool will let you upload a contract (PDF/DOCX/TXT) and receive:
- A prioritized **risk list** with severity
- **Suggested language** (goal + fallback)
- A concise **summary** and next steps

> **Status:** UI and backend wiring come in the next step.  
> **Disclaimer:** AI-assisted. Informational only. Not legal advice.
